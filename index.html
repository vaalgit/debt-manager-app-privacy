FAQ / 常見問題

How do I add a stock? / 怎麼新增股票？

Tap to add a holding, then type the ticker code (e.g. 2330, 0050, 00631L) or the Chinese name (e.g. 台積電) in the symbol field and pick from the dropdown. ／ 在代號欄輸入股票代號（如 2330、0050、00631L）或中文名（如 台積電），從下拉選單點選即可。

A price isn't showing — why? / 抓不到股價？

Live quotes come from Yahoo Finance's public endpoint. Listed/ETF symbols use .TW and OTC (上櫃) symbols use .TWO automatically. Double-check the code is correct; occasionally the quote source is briefly unavailable. ／ 報價來自 Yahoo 公開端點，上市/ETF 用 .TW、上櫃自動用 .TWO。請確認代號正確；偶爾報價來源會短暫無法連線。

How do I unlock the Stress Test? / 怎麼解鎖斷頭壓力測試？

It's a one-time in-app purchase (Pro unlock). After buying, it stays unlocked. Reinstalled or on a new device? Use Restore Purchases on the unlock screen. ／ 那是一次性內購（Pro 解鎖），購買後永久解鎖。重裝或換機請在解鎖頁按「恢復購買」。

Is my data private? / 我的資料安全嗎？

Yes — your portfolio and numbers are stored only on your device; we have no account system and run no trackers. See our Privacy Policy. ／ 是的，你的投資組合與數字只存在你的裝置上，沒有帳號、沒有追蹤。詳見隱私政策。

Is this investment advice? / 這是投資建議嗎？

No. Pledge Master is a personal calculator. All figures are for reference only and are not investment advice; actual margin-call and liquidation rules depend on your broker. ／ 不是。本 App 是個人試算工具，所有數字僅供參考、不構成投資建議；實際追繳與斷頭標準以你的券商規定為準。
