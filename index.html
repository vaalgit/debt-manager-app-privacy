Privacy Policy — Pledge Master（質押大師）

Effective date / 生效日期: 2026/06/28  ·  Contact / 聯絡: chihyuanchou@gmail.com

English

Summary. Pledge Master is a personal stock-pledge / leverage portfolio tool. Your financial data stays on your device. We do not run analytics, tracking, or advertising, and we have no account system.

What stays on your device

Your portfolio, positions, debts, cash records and settings are stored locally on your device (an on-device database). They are not uploaded to us and we cannot see them.

Third parties we contact (only what's needed to work)

  • Yahoo Finance — to show live quotes, the stock symbols you track are sent to Yahoo's public quote endpoint. We do not send your identity or your portfolio. Yahoo's own privacy terms apply to that request.
  • In-app feedback / wishlist (optional). If you choose to send feedback, the text you type is sent to the developer (via a Google Apps Script endpoint and/or an email you initiate). This is voluntary and only happens when you tap send. Don't include sensitive information you don't want shared.
  • Tips via Ko‑fi (Android only, optional). On Android, an optional "tip" button opens Ko‑fi in your browser; any payment there is handled by Ko‑fi under their terms. The iOS version does not use Ko‑fi.
  • Purchases & tips. The one-time Pro unlock and the optional in-app tips are processed by Apple via the App Store. We never see or store your payment details.

What we do NOT do

No analytics SDKs, no third-party trackers, no advertising, no selling of data, no user accounts, no background location.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.

Your control

Deleting the app removes the on-device data. For feedback you've sent, contact us at the email above.

Changes

We may update this policy; the effective date above will change. Material changes will be noted in the app or release notes.

繁體中文

摘要。質押大師是個人股票質押／槓桿部位的管理工具。你的財務資料 留在你的裝置上。我們不做任何分析、追蹤或廣告，也沒有帳號系統。

留在你裝置上的資料

你的投資組合、部位、負債、現金紀錄與設定，都儲存在你裝置本機的資料庫裡， 不會上傳給我們，我們也看不到。

我們會連線的第三方（只為了讓功能運作）

  • Yahoo Finance — 為了顯示即時報價，會把你追蹤的股票代號 送到 Yahoo 的公開報價端點。我們不會送出你的身分或投資組合內容。該請求適用 Yahoo 自己的隱私條款。
  • App 內意見／許願（選用）。若你選擇送出意見，你輸入的文字 會傳給開發者（透過 Google Apps Script 端點，和／或由你自己發起的 email）。這完全是自願的， 只有在你按下送出時才會發生；請勿填入你不想分享的敏感資訊。
  • Ko‑fi 打賞（僅 Android、選用）。在 Android 版，選用的「打賞」按鈕會在 瀏覽器開啟 Ko‑fi；該處的付款由 Ko‑fi 依其條款處理。iOS 版不使用 Ko‑fi。
  • 購買與打賞。一次性 Pro 解鎖與選用的 App 內打賞，皆由 Apple 透過 App Store 處理，我們不會看到也不會儲存你的付款資訊。

我們不會做的事

沒有分析 SDK、沒有第三方追蹤、沒有廣告、不販售資料、沒有使用者帳號、不在背景取得位置。

兒童

本 App 並非以 13 歲以下兒童為對象。

你的控制權

刪除 App 即可移除裝置上的資料。已送出的意見，請來信上方信箱聯絡。

變更

我們可能更新本政策；更新時上方生效日期會改變，重大變更會在 App 內或更新說明中告知。
